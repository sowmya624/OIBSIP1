Dennis Ritchie

1941-2011

Biography

Dennis Ritchie (1941-2011) was an American computer scientist known for creating the C programming language and co-developing the UNIX operating system. C, designed for system software, became widely adopted and influenced the development of other languages. Ritchie's work on UNIX laid the foundation for modern operating systems. He received the Turing Award in 1983 for his contributions. Ritchie's enduring legacy continues to shape computer science and revolutionize programming.
